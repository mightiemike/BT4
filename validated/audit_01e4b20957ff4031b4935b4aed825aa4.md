### Title
`AllResources(l1_gas=X, l2_gas=0, l1_data_gas=0)` silently downcasts to `L1Gas(X)` in protobuf deserialization, causing hash-domain divergence and p2p rejection of gateway-accepted transactions — (File: `crates/apollo_protobuf/src/converters/transaction.rs`)

---

### Summary

The `TryFrom<protobuf::ResourceBounds> for ValidResourceBounds` conversion silently produces `ValidResourceBounds::L1Gas` when both `l2_gas` and `l1_data_gas` are zero. The gateway's stateless validator explicitly accepts `InvokeV3` transactions with only `l1_gas` non-zero (`AllResourceBounds{l1_gas=X, l2_gas=0, l1_data_gas=0}`). The hash for such a transaction is computed using the 3-element `get_tip_resource_bounds_hash` path (L1_GAS, L2_GAS, L1_DATA_GAS). When the same transaction is serialized to protobuf and deserialized by any peer (mempool p2p or consensus), the downcast to `L1Gas` causes `TryFrom<InvokeTransactionV3> for RpcInvokeTransactionV3` to fail with `DEPRECATED_RESOURCE_BOUNDS_ERROR`, rejecting the transaction entirely. This creates a split: the proposer sequenced the transaction under a 3-element hash; every other node rejects it before sequencing.
