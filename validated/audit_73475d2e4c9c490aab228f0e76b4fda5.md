### Title
Resharding Manager Inherits Parent's `proposed_split` into Child `ChunkExtra`, Causing `InvalidChunkHeaderShardSplit` at Every Dynamic-Resharding Epoch Boundary — (File: `chain/chain/src/resharding/manager.rs`)

---

### Summary

When a shard is split under `ProtocolFeature::DynamicResharding`, the resharding manager initialises each child shard's `ChunkExtra` by cloning the parent's `ChunkExtra` and updating only `state_root` and `congestion_info`. The `proposed_split` field introduced in `ChunkExtraV5` is never reset to `None`. Because the parent's final chunk always carries a non-`None` `proposed_split` (that is precisely the value the block producer used to trigger the split), every child shard starts life with a stale, non-`None` `proposed_split` in its stored `ChunkExtra`. The child's first real chunk header, however, computes `proposed_split = None` (the resharding cooldown prevents an immediate re-split). The strict equality check in `validate_chunk_with_chunk_extra_and_receipts_root` then rejects every chunk the child shard ever produces, permanently stalling it.
