### Title
`ValidResourceBounds` Variant Incorrectly Reconstructed from Protobuf Produces a Different Transaction Hash — (`File: crates/apollo_protobuf/src/converters/transaction.rs`)

---

### Summary

The protobuf-to-Rust conversion for `ValidResourceBounds` uses a zero-value heuristic to decide between the `L1Gas` (pre-0.13.3) and `AllResources` (post-0.13.3) variants. Because the transaction-hash function `get_tip_resource_bounds_hash` produces structurally different output for the two variants — even when the numeric bounds are identical — a post-0.13.3 `DeployAccountTransactionV3` or `DeclareTransactionV3` whose L2Gas and L1DataGas bounds are both zero will be reconstructed with the wrong variant after a protobuf round-trip, yielding a different transaction hash than the one committed to the block.
