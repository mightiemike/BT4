### Title
`ValidResourceBounds` protobuf round-trip silently downgrades `AllResources{l2=0,l1_data=0}` to `L1Gas`, causing consensus proposal rejection — (File: `crates/apollo_protobuf/src/converters/transaction.rs`)

---

### Summary

The `TryFrom<protobuf::ResourceBounds> for ValidResourceBounds` conversion uses a zero-value heuristic to distinguish between the `L1Gas` and `AllResources` enum variants. A valid `RpcInvokeTransactionV3` carrying `AllResourceBounds { l1_gas: X, l2_gas: 0, l1_data_gas: 0 }` is accepted by the gateway and hashed under the `AllResources` domain (which includes `L1_DATA_GAS` in the preimage). When the proposer serialises this transaction into a consensus protobuf message and a peer deserialises it, the heuristic silently converts the resource bounds back to `ValidResourceBounds::L1Gas(X)`. The downstream `TryFrom<InvokeTransactionV3> for RpcInvokeTransactionV3` then hard-fails with `DEPRECATED_RESOURCE_BOUNDS
